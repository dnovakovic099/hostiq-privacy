Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect my property management system?
Navigate to Settings → Integrations in the app. Select your PMS from the list and follow the authentication steps. We support major platforms including Hostaway, Guesty, and more.
How do I add a new property?
Tap the + button on the Properties tab, enter your property details, and save. You can also import properties directly from your connected PMS.
How does AI pricing work?
Our AI analyzes market data, seasonal trends, local events, and competitor pricing to suggest optimal rates for your properties. You can review and approve suggestions before they're applied.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can manage your subscription through your device's App Store or Play Store settings. Go to Settings → Subscriptions → HostIQ to cancel or modify your plan.
Is my data secure?
Yes! We use industry-standard encryption for all data in transit and at rest. Your property and financial information is protected with bank-level security measures.
How do I report a bug?
Please email us at support@hostiq.app with a description of the issue, steps to reproduce it, and your device model and iOS/Android version. Screenshots are helpful!
